# Sample Data Note

CollectVoice AI is a public mock/demo MVP. Any sample data used in this repository must be fake and suitable for public demos.

Do not commit:

- Real customer names.
- Real phone numbers.
- Real loan IDs, agreement IDs, account numbers, or case references.
- Real lender files.
- Real agency portfolio data.
- Real payment commitments.
- Real call transcripts or recordings.
- API keys, secrets, credentials, or provider tokens.

Acceptable sample data:

- Clearly fake customer names.
- Clearly fake phone numbers.
- Clearly fake case references such as `CVAI-DEMO-LN-1001`.
- Fake lenders or fictional NBFC/bank names.
- Fake outstanding amounts, DPD values, and promise-to-pay records.
- Mock transcripts generated only for demo purposes.

Before sharing or committing new sample files, check that the data cannot identify a real borrower, lender portfolio, collection agency, or phone number owner.
